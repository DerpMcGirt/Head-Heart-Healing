Get to know your therapist!

Denise is a compassionate and experienced therapist who brings a wealth of life experience to her practice. Having navigated various personal challenges, including marriage, parenting, divorce, and blended families, she understands the complexities of relationships and personal growth.

Denise has a positive outlook on life and values genuine conversations that build trust and therapeutic relationships. She has strong, supportive relationships with her adult children and their partners, and she discovered her true calling later in life, realizing her gift extends beyond making great appetizers for parties.

Denise loves to laugh and often uses humor as a coping mechanism. She enjoys learning about a wide range of topics and is spiritual, holding firm beliefs without imposing them on others.

In her sessions, Denise uses analogies and references from movies, books, music, and social media to connect with her clients. She focuses on building rapport and trust, allowing clients to express their thoughts and feelings openly. Denise helps clients explore their goals, whether they seek a different perspective, validation, or simply someone to listen.

Therapeutic Approach

Denise's approach is client-centered and flexible, tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. She encourages clients to share their thoughts and feelings, offering guidance when needed. Together, they explore questions about self-improvement, emotional well-being, and life's challenges.

Specialties and Interests

  • Trauma: Utilizing effective protocols to aid in healing and understanding individual experiences.
  • Dissociative Identity Disorder
  • Life Changes: Addressing lack of life satisfaction and questioning purpose.
  • Relationships: Supporting alternative lifestyles, intimacy, codependency, and domestic violence.
  • LGBTQIA+ Affirming Therapy
  • Spirituality: Exploring beliefs and addressing religious trauma.
  • Denise is dedicated to continual education and staying informed about the latest therapeutic practices to provide the best care for her clients.
